Corte plasma y maquinados Querétaro
Small company

Corte Plasma CNC Querétaro is a company founded in 2025 in collaboration with Misael Reséndiz. It specializes in CNC plasma cutting for materials such as carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum. The company operates through its web platform https://corteqro.com/, where customers can upload their 2D CAD models. Using an integrated API, the system automatically calculates the cost of each design, enabling clients to generate an order based on their specifications. Once the order is confirmed, the design is processed and converted into G-code to perform the cutting operations on the CNC machine.
